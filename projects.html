Projects

Some of the major projects in which RuyBon team has been involved:

Hezaro Yek Shahr is a cultural, recreational, sport and retail project which is being constructed in a land of 138ha in west of Tehran, District 22.

This complex contains a theme park, Retail Mall, Water Park, 3 star hotel, 4 star hotel and also a 5 star hotel. The project also includes administrative and office towers, Etc.

Hezaro Yek Shahr has enjoyed the designs of top ranked international consultants and the RuyBon team has been in close cooperation with those consultants and also Iranian engineering firms.

Team members of RuyBon worked and were involved in TisaKish1 Retail mall (268 shops with approximate 67,000 sqm area), TisaKish2 Retail mall (388 shops with approximate 85,000 sqm area) and also Didar Residential complex; all in Kish island in Persian Gulf.

Tehran Digital Arts Park in AbbasAbad lands in heart of Tehran is designed for a 40,000 sqm land. The mission of this park is to improve the capability of animation production, Cinema Visual effect, advertisement tools and computer games and it will be active in Training and production sectors.

Team members of RuyBon were involved in the Technical and constructional department of this project.

Anzab tunnel in Tajikistan with the approximate length of 5Km had been built and operated as a 52M.USD project in 2004.

RuyBon members were involved in highest technical levels of this project.

A Project of the Uzbekistan's ministry of Statistics and Macroeconomics, financed by World Bank and supervised by Netherland's NEDECO

The project included 27Km of cast iron pipes, two water tank towers in height of 36 meters and capacity of 500Cubic meters, two ground tanks of water and one station of pumping and chlorination. The project costs 8.8 M.USD.

Golestan dam is built in 12Km distance of the North East of the GonbadKavoos city in north of Iran.

The height from foundation is 25m and is 17m from the river's bed. Crown length of the dam is 1367m and the capacity of reservoir is 56Million Cubic Meters approximately.

The eastern tower of Esteghlal Hotel (Parsian Esteghlal) was in urgent need of a full refurbishing in high luxurious levels just before the Meetings of the Islamic cooperation union.

This very special project has been completely done by RuyBon members and the gained experiences of that project is now one of RuyBon's most valuable capitals.
